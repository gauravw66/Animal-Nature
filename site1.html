What is Carbon Footprint ?

Ecological Footprint

The amount of land and water needed to produces resources for mankind
and absorb the waste produced.

Carbon Footprint

The amount of CO2 released into the atmosphere due to human activity.
Considered to be a part of the Ecological Footprint.
